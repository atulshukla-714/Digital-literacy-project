# Task 5 – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## 🛡️ Stay Safe Online

### 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Never share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
- Do not click on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Always verify official websites before entering personal details.
- Avoid installing unknown apps or screen-sharing apps on request.
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

### 💳 UPI / Financial Safety
- Entering UPI PIN means you are paying money, not receiving it.
- Do not accept or approve unknown payment requests.
- Always verify QR codes before scanning them.

### 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

## 🧠 Reflection

While researching cybercrime, I was most surprised by how easily scammers manipulate people using simple tricks like fake customer care numbers and urgency. I realized that even educated individuals can fall victim if they are not careful. The fact that entering a UPI PIN always means sending money was something I had not fully understood before.

This task made me more aware of digital safety and the importance of verifying information before taking action. One habit I will personally change is avoiding quick decisions during online transactions and always double-checking payment requests and sources. I will also spread awareness among my friends and family so they do not become victims of such scams.
